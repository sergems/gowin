====================================================
CASH OUT OVERVIEW
====================================================

Cash Out allows a customer to sell their ticket back to the sportsbook before the ticket is fully settled.

When a customer accepts Cash Out:

- The ticket immediately becomes CLOSED.
- The customer receives the offered amount instantly into their wallet.
- The ticket can never win again.
- Remaining events no longer affect that ticket.

====================================================
WHEN CASH OUT IS AVAILABLE
====================================================

Cash Out should only be available when ALL of the following conditions are true:

✓ Ticket status is Active
✓ Ticket has not already been Cashed Out
✓ Ticket has not already Lost
✓ Ticket has not already Won
✓ Ticket has at least one unsettled selection
✓ Cash Out is globally enabled
✓ Sport is enabled for Cash Out
✓ Ticket type is eligible
✓ Live odds are available for all remaining selections

Cash Out should automatically disappear if:

- Any remaining event has suspended odds
- Event is postponed
- Event is cancelled
- Live odds unavailable
- Admin disables Cash Out
- Ticket already settled

====================================================
CASH OUT CALCULATION
====================================================

The calculation must NEVER use the original ticket odds.

It must use CURRENT LIVE ODDS.

Process:

Step 1

Determine all remaining unsettled selections.

Step 2

Convert current live odds into implied probability.

Example:

Decimal Odds = 1.50

Probability

1 / 1.50

=66.67%

Do this for every remaining selection.

Step 3

Multiply all remaining probabilities.

Example

Selection A

80%

Selection B

65%

Combined Probability

0.80 × 0.65

=52%

Step 4

Fair Value

Potential Payout × Combined Probability

Example

Potential payout

$500

Combined probability

52%

Fair value

$260

Step 5

Apply the House Margin

Offer

Fair Value × (1 - House Margin)

Example

Fair value

$260

House Margin

10%

Offer

$234

Display

Cash Out Available

$234
