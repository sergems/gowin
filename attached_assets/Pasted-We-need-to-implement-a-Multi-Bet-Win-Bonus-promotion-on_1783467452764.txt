Bonus Calculation

Bonus is calculated using the total winnings, not the stake.

Formula:

Base Win = Stake × Combined Odds

Bonus Amount = Base Win × (Bonus Percentage / 100)

Potential Win = Base Win + Bonus Amount

Example

Stake:

$20

Combined Odds:

150.00

Base Win:

20 × 150 = 3,000

Qualifying selections:

50

Bonus:

1250%

Bonus Amount:

3,000 × 12.5 = 37,500

Potential Win:

40,500

Maximum Win

Maximum payout is

USD 1,000,000

After bonus calculation:

Final Potential Win =
MIN(Base Win + Bonus Amount, 1,000,000)

If capped, display:

Maximum payout of USD 1,000,000 applied.

Important Business Rules
Bonus applies only to accumulator (multi) bets.
Only selections with decimal odds greater than 1.50 count toward the bonus qualification.
Selections with odds of 1.50 or below still contribute to the combined odds but do not count toward the bonus percentage.
Bonus starts at 10 qualifying selections.
Maximum selections allowed is 50.
Maximum bonus is 1250% at 50 qualifying selections.
The bonus is calculated on the base winnings (Stake × Combined Odds), not on the stake.
The final payout is Base Win + Bonus Amount, subject to a maximum payout cap of USD 1,000,000.
The backend must perform all calculations and validations to prevent manipulation.
The promotion should be fully configurable through the admin panel without requiring code changes.
